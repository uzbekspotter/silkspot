This Privacy Policy describes how **SILKSPOT** (**“we”**, **“us”**) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and services (**“Service”**).

## 1. Information we collect

**Account data:** When you register, we collect your email address, username, and credentials. You may also add a display name, avatar, and other profile fields.

**Content:** Photos you upload, titles, descriptions, categories, and related metadata (e.g. aircraft registration, airport, date) are stored to operate the Service.

**Usage data:** We may collect technical information such as browser type, approximate region (from IP), and logs needed for security, debugging, and aggregate analytics.

## 2. How we use information

- To create and maintain your account and authenticate you.
- To display your profile and uploads to other users according to product rules.
- To operate moderation, safety, and abuse prevention.
- To improve reliability and performance of the Service.
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our [Terms of Service](/terms).

## 3. Where data is processed

The Service is built on **Supabase** (database and authentication) and may use **object storage** (e.g. cloud storage) for images. Processors may be located in the EU, US, or other regions depending on configuration. By using the Service, you acknowledge that data may be transferred to countries where you live or where our providers operate.

## 4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

- Service providers who help us run the platform (hosting, email delivery, storage), under appropriate agreements.
- Authorities when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Aggregated or de-identified statistics may be shown publicly (e.g. upload counts).

## 5. Retention

We retain account and content data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for backups, legal compliance, and dispute resolution. You may request account deletion subject to legal retention needs.

## 6. Your choices

- Update profile data in **Settings** where available.
- Use sign-in and password tools we provide (including password reset).
- Contact us to exercise access, correction, or deletion rights where applicable in your jurisdiction.

## 7. Cookies and local storage

We use cookies or similar technologies for session management (staying signed in) and security. Essential cookies are required for the Service to function.

## 8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from young children.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy. The **Last updated** date will change; continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy where permitted by law.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options listed on the site (e.g. **About** or community channels).
